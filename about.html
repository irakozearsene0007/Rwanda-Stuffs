Our Story

Rwanda Cinema was born from a simple observation: while Rwandan cinema was thriving locally, it remained largely inaccessible to the global audience. Many classic Kinyarwanda films were at risk of being lost, and new productions struggled to reach international viewers.

Founded in 2024, we set out to create a digital bridge connecting Rwanda's vibrant entertainment industry with audiences worldwide. Our platform combines modern streaming technology with deep respect for cultural authenticity.

Today, we're proud to host a growing collection of Rwandan movies, comedy series like "Papa Sava," drama films, music videos, and documentaries - all available for free to celebrate and share Rwanda's unique storytelling traditions.
